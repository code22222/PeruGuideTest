My Review

Lima makes up 11 million out of Peru's 16 million population, and being Peru's capital, has plenty to offer, however, it felt a bit too city-like for my taste. While I enjoyed exploring the main square (Plaza de Armas), Miraflores, and Barranco, I wouldn't spend more than 2-3 days here.

Itinerary

Day 1

  • Flight from EWR to LIM arrived late at night
  • Took a *taxi* from the airport to the hostel
  • My first and only encounter with the Peruvian taxi system ended with me being overcharged 3x the taxi fare because the currency was never established. Taxi drivers are very sneaky and use techniques to scam tourists so stay away from them

Day 2

  • Explored Miraflores, including various parks and neighborhoods
  • Visited Gaudi Park, enjoying the scenery as I watched surfers and a paraglider along the coast
  • Visited Barranco and explored the surrounding area
  • Went on a free walking tour through my hostel
  • Saw Peruvian favelas (dangerous neighborhoods) from a distance
  • Rode a Combi with the mini-bus moving while the doors were still open
  • Tried chocolate-filled churros, Pisco sour shots, and browsed a local souvenir shop
  • Visited the beutiful main plaza (Plaza de Armas) and saw multiple churches in the surrounding area
  • Ended the night with a colorful water fountain light show with the new friends made on the walking tour

Accommodations

1900 Backpacker Hostel

Private single room with shared bathroom: $20/night x 2 nights = $40
Check-In: May 8th (2PM - 11PM)
Check-Out: May 10th (until 11AM)
Free Cancellation Before May 3rd
  • This hostel wasn’t too special compared to the other hostels I stayed in during my trip, but the free walking tour was great, not only because of the information and sights, but because I made a lot of new friends

Excursions & Activities

Water Fountain Light Show

Price: $1.50
  • This was a very cool and inexpensive activity that is well worth the visit. Not only is it cool to watch the fountains and the various changes in colors and pressure, but around 9pm (times vary), there is a light show where short animations are displayed onto the fountains. This is definitely a unique experience I enjoyed…and it was cheap!
